One part personal passion project, another part potential portfolio piece. This was created as a place to catalogue and display my wildlife and astrophotography pictures, while

how?

Having never owned a camera, I was hesistant on investing too much into something that may have only been a brief distraction. As such, all of my kit is entry/mid old second hand kit from MPB. To fill out the catalogue, I've also included some pictures from a previous phone, but hope to replace with better pictures when given the chance.

  • Canon EOS 70D
  • Canon EF 200mm f/2.8 L II USM
  • Samyang MF 14mm f/2.8 MK2
  • Samsung Galaxy S23 FE

when?

As an escape from university coursework, I found myself enjoying YouTube's amateur astronomers and their budget builds for entry into astrophotography. I soon found myself trying to investigate the skies with what little equipment I had. Attempting long exposure pictures of the stars with little more than my old phone and aligning a cheap pair of binoculars with my phone's camera to take pictures of the moon.
