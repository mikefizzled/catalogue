saturn

Clearly pushing my cheap setup to its limit, I was able to picture Saturn and a few moons. Saturn also has a huge number of moons with 146 currently known. Titan, the largest moon of Saturn can clearly be observed. Rhea appears visible, too, along with the largest of Saturn's outer moons, Iapetus. A surprise was the capture of the StarLink 1226 satellite. As a longer exposure was used to try collect enough light for the moons, the satellite is seen as a bright streak.

northern lights

Having missed the majority solar storm, I only managed to get a handful of pictures of a dancing purple sky. I wrongly assumed it wasn't visible due to light pollution on our road. During the event, I also captured the blood moon crescent that was briefly visible.
